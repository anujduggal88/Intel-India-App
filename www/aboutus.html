About Intel India

Intel India Profile

  • Intel India is Intel’s largest non-manufacturing site outside the US; a microcosm of Intel with presence of all major business groups - PEG, CCG, IT, TMG, DCG, Intel Labs, SMG, SSG, Biz enabling groups.
  • Started operations with a sales office in 1988 and research & development activities commenced in 1999
  • R&D center located in Bengaluru & Pune with Sales teams across 6 locations in India
  • Investment in India till date is ~ US$ 3 Billion
  • Intel India has grown from 200 employees in 2000 to >6800 employees now
  • Critical engineering center  with competencies across SoC, graphics, platform and software (from high-end Intel® Xeon® servers to 2 in 1s, tablets, IoT & wearables)
  • 56% of employee contributed to community volunteering activities in 2014

  • Location: SRR, BA, India
